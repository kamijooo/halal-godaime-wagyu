Five generations · 五代
Why Skytree · この店について

The wagyu we'd serve our family.

家族に出せる和牛を、あなたへ。

Five generations ago, a small Tokyo butcher made his sons one promise — never sell meat he wouldn't feed his own family. Three wars, sixty-three years, and forty-seven countries of guests later, that rule still runs this kitchen.

Our Skytree restaurant opened to share that wagyu with travelers — and because so many of our guests are Muslim, we rebuilt the entire kitchen around halal certification. Same family rule. Same cleaver. Wider table.

"Wagyu shouldn't have borders." — 四代目, said in 2014

Halal · ハラル認証

Certified, top to bottom.

仕入れから調理まで、ハラル認証。

  • 100% halal-certified Japanese wagyu — no exceptions, all cuts.
  • No alcohol in any sauce, marinade, or dashi. We reformulated every recipe.
  • Separated cookware — color-coded, audited monthly.
  • Original certificate framed at the counter. Digital copy on request.
